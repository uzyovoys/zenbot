What is Zenbot

Zenbot is all-in-one solution for building chatbot or natural language interface for your app or website.

Instead of making everything on your own, you can use Zenbot which provides NLP tools and bot hosting in a single service. Moreover, it is already connected with Facebook Messenger, Slack, Telegram and other channels! Thus you have not to implement all of these messengers APIs.

Who can develop with Zenbot

Zenbot provides a chatbot framework for developers who familiar with programming a bit, but didn\'t dive into NLP or AI yet.

You can use your own Javascript code thus it is better if you have some experience with it. But it is not mandatory - you can still create a great bots without programming.

What is difference?

Zenbot is different from most of other chatbot platforms:

  • Zenbot is not just an Intent-resolver or graphical bot builder. It provides an all-in-one solution enabling you to develop even very complex bots.
  • Zenbot is already integrated with Facebook Messenger, Slack, Skype, Telegram, Kik and WeChat messengers. Thus you don't need to implement all APIs on your own to make your bot available for millions.
  • Your bot won't need to pass a Facebook app review process if you would like to publish it in Facebook. Just three clicks and your chatbot is published!
  • You don't have to have an external server to host your chatbot's logic - Zenbot does it for you.
  • There is an extremely flexible and powerful NLP engine which helps you to build a really conversational bots.
  • You are not limited with text only. You can use both text and buttons and teach you bot to react on button clicks.
  • You are not limited with Zenbot's message format in every output message of your bot - your bot can generate a native outputs of every messenger you use.
  • You can use a well-known Javascript code to develop the most advanced logic for your bot.
  • Zenbot is not limited with messengers only. Each your bot is available through a REST API, thus you can easily conndect it to any of your apps or websites.
  • Zenbot doesn't provide any proprietary analytics tool. Instead it is integrated with the best solutions on the market like Mixpanel, Google Analytics and others to provide you the best analytics experience.
  • Zenbot doesn't provide any graphical bot builder. Instead it gives you a freedom of hacking!
  • And yes - it is FREE to use
